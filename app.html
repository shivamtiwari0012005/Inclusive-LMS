Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this course for?

This course is designed for anyone interested in becoming a data scientist, including complete beginners. If you have some programming or math background, you'll progress faster, but we cover all the fundamentals you need.

What are the prerequisites?

The only prerequisites are basic computer skills and high school level math. We start from the very basics of programming and statistics before moving to advanced topics.

How much time should I dedicate weekly?

We recommend dedicating 10-15 hours per week to complete the course in 6 months. The self-paced option allows you to move faster or slower as needed.
